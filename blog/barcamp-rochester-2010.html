BarCamp Rochester 2010

Fri 22 October 2010
By interlockroc

BarCamp Rochester will be taking place at the Rochester Institute Of Technology tomorrow, Saturday, October 23rd. A number of Interlock members will be giving presentations on topics including information security, HAM radio, making ginger beer and more. Look for our table at the event for some schwag and to learn more about Interlock.

For more information about BarCamp or to get directions, visit the BarCamp Rochester site.
